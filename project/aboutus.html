Dr. Glen Jukes, D.C.

Glen's introduction to the world of gaming came during an Boy Scout Encampment at Camp Pendleton many years ago. One of his fellow Scouts had brought a Dungeons and Dragons game, the old one with the blue dice. Needless to say, Glen was hooked. Now many years later, Glen still games with some of those fellows on the regular.

David Jukes

It may come as no surprise that as the younger brother, David was introduced to gaming through his older brother Glen. The brothers spent countless hours during their youth delving dungeons and living in worlds of fantasy. The shared experiences have formed a bond that will last eternity.
