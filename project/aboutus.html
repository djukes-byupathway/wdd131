About Us

Our Story

At the end of 2025, one of our founders, Glen Jukes collapsed at work with a stroke caused by a worn out mechanical heart valve. To help assist in the mental and physical recovery from both the stroke and high risk heart surgery (btw the crew at BSW Heart Hospital in Plano Texas are the best!!!) we decided to collaborate on something we share and love, gaming. Vault of Jukes is specifically about using our love for gaming, fantasy, and friendship to promote well-being. We hope you join us in our passion for life and exploring the depths of possibility and imagination.

Meet the Founders

Dr. Glen Jukes, D.C.

Glen's introduction to the world of gaming came during an Boy Scout Encampment at Camp Pendleton many years ago. One of his fellow Scouts had brought a Dungeons and Dragons game, the old one with the blue dice. Needless to say, Glen was hooked. Now many years later, Glen still games with some of those fellows on the regular.

David Jukes

It may come as no surprise that as the younger brother, David was introduced to gaming through his older brother Glen. The brothers spent countless hours during their youth delving dungeons and living in worlds of fantasy. The shared experiences have formed a bond that will last eternity.
